Equipment & System Cyclone Scrubber
Cyclone Scrubber
  • Very Simple Machine Structure Produces High Air-Water Contact Efficiency
Principle

  • Utilize Centrifugal Force of Inlet Gas And Central Water Spray To Produce Mixing, Adsorbing, Absorbing, To Remove Pollutants From Exhaust Gas.

Applications

  • Vapor Air Absorption, Mist / Fume Removal, Flue Gas Pre-Treatment.
